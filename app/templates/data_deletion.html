Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data from Radix Auto Reply, please contact us through the app dashboard or send an email to the contact email associated with your Meta Developer account.

Upon receiving your request, we will delete all stored configuration data, including access tokens, campaign settings, and analytics logs, within 30 days.

Alternatively, you can disconnect your social media accounts at any time through the dashboard settings, which will remove all associated data.
