Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Radix Auto Reply ("we", "our", "the App") is a social media auto-reply tool that helps businesses manage Instagram and Facebook interactions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your Facebook Page and Instagram Business Account to our App, we collect:

  • Page and Profile Information: Public page name, ID, profile picture, and page access tokens.
  • Comments and Messages: Instagram and Facebook comments and messages directed at your business page, solely for the purpose of auto-replying based on your configured campaigns.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated metrics about replies sent, messages sent, and error counts.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide automated replies to comments and messages on your connected social media pages.
  • To display analytics and activity logs within your dashboard.
  • To improve and maintain the App's functionality.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your configuration data (campaigns, keywords, reply templates, access tokens) in an encrypted database. Access tokens are stored securely and are never shared with third parties. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your configuration data for as long as your account is active. Processed comments and logs are retained for up to 90 days. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

Our App interacts with Meta Platforms (Facebook and Instagram) via their official Graph API. Your data is processed in accordance with Meta's Platform Terms. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

7. Data Sharing

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram): The App communicates with Meta's servers to read and respond to comments/messages. This is inherent to the service's functionality.
  • Hosting Provider: The App is hosted on Railway, which processes data solely for infrastructure purposes.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may disconnect your social media accounts at any time through the dashboard settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app dashboard or at the email associated with your Meta Developer account.
